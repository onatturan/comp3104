George Brown College is a public college located in Toronto, Ontario.
 It was established in 1967 and offers a wide range of programs in fields such as business, 
 health sciences, culinary arts, design, and technology. The college provides 
 practical learning opportunities through co-ops and internships to help students prepare
  for successful careers after graduation. With over 30,000 students from more than 120 countries,
   it offers a diverse and inclusive environment.
